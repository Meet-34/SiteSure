# SiteAudit 🏗️

SiteAudit is a full-stack construction quality assurance and site monitoring platform developed to digitize daily construction site reporting and inspection management.

The platform helps engineers and construction teams record daily updates, monitor project progress, maintain accountability, and improve quality assurance processes directly from the site.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📋 Daily Site Update Logging
- 🏗️ Construction Quality Assurance Tracking
- 📸 Site Image & Evidence Upload
- 📊 Real-Time Progress Monitoring
- 📝 Inspection & Reporting System
- 🔍 Centralized Construction Records
- 👷 Team Accountability & Collaboration
- ☁️ Cloud Deployment using Render

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Construction sites often rely on manual methods for:
- Quality checks
- Material tracking
- Site reporting
- Progress monitoring

This leads to:
- Missing or scattered data
- Delayed reporting
- Poor visibility of site activities
- Lack of accountability
- Increased chances of errors and rework

SiteAudit solves these problems by providing a centralized digital platform for construction monitoring and quality assurance.

## ⚙️ How SiteAudit Works

1. Engineers log daily construction updates
2. Site inspections and observations are recorded
3. Images and reports are uploaded to the system
4. Data is stored centrally for easy access
5. Teams can monitor project quality and progress in real time
